# Dungeon-Crawler-C++

A tiny C++ dungeon crawler that turns your terminal into a small but adventurous world. Explore dark corners, fight mysterious creatures, and uncover hidden secrets as you progress step by step. With simple ASCII visuals and a charming minimalist style, it focuses on pure gameplay, where every decision matters and even the smallest adventure can become a legend. This project also serves as a way for me to deepen my understanding of C++, especially working with threads, structs, classes, and core programming concepts.
